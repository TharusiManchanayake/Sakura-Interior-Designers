Meet the Sakura Design Team

Our experienced team of designers brings international expertise, thoughtful craft, and a personal approach to every project.

Isabella Reed
Principal Designer & Founder

Isabella founded Sakura Interior Designers after 15 years in the global interior design industry. A graduate of Parsons School of Design in New York, she is known for her refined approach that blends contemporary minimalism with timeless elegance. Her leadership and creative direction drive Sakura’s reputation for excellence, balance, and beauty.

Aiko Tanaka
Creative Design Lead

Aiko, originally from Japan, holds a Master’s in Interior Architecture from Kyoto University of Art and Design. With over 10 years of international experience, he’s recognized for his innovative designs that merge clean, modern aesthetics with subtle Japanese influence. His creative vision brings a sense of harmony and artistic flair to every project.

Olivia Chen
Senior Interior Architect

With a Master’s in Interior Architecture from the University of Melbourne and over 12 years of experience, Olivia specializes in high-end residential and commercial spaces. Her designs are celebrated for their thoughtful functionality, soft modernism, and refined detailing that enhance everyday living.

Michael Tan
Commercial Design Expert

Michael earned his Master’s in Interior Design from the Royal College of Art, London. With 11 years in the field, he has led major office, retail, and hospitality projects. His work focuses on balancing brand storytelling with practical design to create engaging, purposeful environments.

Ryan Mitchell
Modern Minimalist Designer

Ryan graduated from the Rhode Island School of Design and has 8 years of experience designing minimalist interiors. His philosophy centers on “less but better,” combining simplicity, space, and light to achieve calm, modern interiors that feel effortless and sophisticated.

Grace Patel
Residential Design Specialist

Grace holds a Bachelor’s in Interior and Spatial Design from the London College of Art. With 9 years of residential experience, she creates homes that blend elegance and comfort. Her eye for detail and color allows her to turn modern living spaces into personal sanctuaries.

Ethan Parker
Lighting and Space Designer

Ethan is a certified lighting and spatial designer with 9 years of experience transforming interiors through light and architecture. His expertise lies in crafting atmosphere and emotion — using illumination to enhance depth, character, and mood in every project.

Sofia Ramirez
Sustainable Design Consultant

Sofia graduated from the University of British Columbia with a degree in Environmental Design. She has 8 years of experience creating eco-conscious interiors focused on sustainability, wellness, and natural materials — proving that luxury and sustainability can coexist beautifully.

Emma Davis
Color and Material Consultant

Emma studied at the Savannah College of Art and Design and brings 7 years of expertise in color theory, materials, and texture. She curates palettes that evoke mood and cohesion, ensuring every project feels balanced, expressive, and tailored to the client’s vision.

Lucas Bennett
Furniture and Custom Design Specialist

Lucas studied Furniture & Interior Design at the University of the Arts London and has 6 years of experience designing bespoke pieces. His custom creations elevate interiors through craftsmanship, proportion, and a perfect blend of function and artistry.
